you have the patience to deal with anything thrown at you, and you need to apply that to your feelings for other people, too. force yourself to see reality. realize when you're spiraling into absurdist and irrational thought. force yourself to wait. you have to. i know this feels artificial, but it doesn't matter. stop thinking so much. you're killing your ability to feel anything. to enjoy anything. to take anything seriously. you can be okay. you will be. you will be.. you will be...
